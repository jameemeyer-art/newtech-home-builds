Who We Are

A Family-Founded Business With a National Reputation

NewTech Home Builds was founded in 2005 by brothers Daniel and Marcus Reid, two tradespeople with a simple belief: that every client deserves the same attention to detail and quality they would put into building their own home.

What started as a small residential building company has grown into a full-service construction and trade business. Today our team of over 45 skilled professionals handles everything from intricate steel fabrication to large-scale residential builds — all under the one roof.

We're still family owned, still locally operated, and still guided by the same values Daniel and Marcus started with: honesty, craftsmanship, and doing the right thing by our clients.
